Compound Interest Calcualtor

What is the compound interest formula?: A=P(1+r/n)(nt) where:

  • A is the total amount calculated
  • P is the principle
  • r is the interest rate
  • n is the number of times interest is applied per unit time
  • t is the number of time periods elapsed

Warning!: The compound interest calculator below will only find the total amount A when all other four elements are inputed. The other compound interest calculator below will find the interest rate r when all other elements are inputed.
